How to pay your regular bills and expenses with Bitcoin?

| | | | | | | |

Originaly posted on Substack on Dec 04, 2022

Updated here on Nov 30, 2024

The nocoiner first question is: "can I pay my electricity bill with Bitcoin?" So here I start putting together a list of places where you can pay your bills with Bitcoin.

This list will be updated periodically. If you know a utility service that is proven to accept Bitcoin just send the link on Telegram @DarthCoin or NOSTR with a simple explanation.

I do not have any relation of affiliation with any of these services, is just a listing of services that accept Bitcoin and many of them are verified with users. No referral links or earning programs involved.

This list is to help users to start using their bitcoins and create the circular economy.

I will invite you first to listen this amazing episode from Once Bitten talking with the guys from Shopinbit about buying everything you want with Bitcoin.

The list is organized in various sections:

  • Intermediaries - they pay fiat bills for you and you pay them in BTC
  • VISA cards providers - you debit a fiat VISA card with your BTC
  • Utility companies that accept BTC directly
  • General products - retailers that accept BTC directly
  • Travel, airplane tickets, rentals that accept BTC directly
  • General merchants lists - public lists with all merchants that accept BTC
  • Cashback / rewards - you do your regular shopping and you get sats as rewards
  • P2P Marketplaces list using Bitcoin

₿ ⚡️ Intermediaries Pay bills with Bitcoin

  • PiixPay.com - pay bills with BTC (EU)
  • Bity.com - pay bills with BTC (CHF/EU)
  • MtPelerin - pay bills with BTC (CHF/EUR)
  • BitcoinWell - Pay bills in US/Canada
  • Bylls - Pay bills in Canada
  • Coins.ph - Pay bills and wages in Asia
  • LivingRoom of Satoshi - pay bills in Australia

  • CoinTree - pay bills in Australia
  • RelayPay - pay bills in Australia
  • PayBTC - pay bills in Australia
  • LightningPay NZ - Buy, sell BTC, pay bills, accept BTC in New Zealand
  • Spritz Finance - pay bills in USA
  • BillBitcoins - pay bills with BTC in India
  • ElectroCoin.hr - payment processor in Croatia for many merchants, with local shops exchanges too.
  • Swapido - swap sats to MXN pesos to any bank account and pay bills.
  • LocalCoinSwap - P2P exchange that can be used for paying bills, with escrow
  • RoboSats Tor - P2P Full private Exchange over Tor, over LN, ask for bill payment in your exchange offer

Visa cards debited by SELLING your bitcoin to them

NOTE: I do not encourage you to use the so called “bitcoin debit cards”. Those are simply fiat VISA cards. You are literally going back to using fiat! But in emergency situations, yes it can be useful.

  • Bitrefill.com - buy vouchers with BTC, phone bills, gas, groceries
  • The Bitcoin Company - buy gift cards BTC, phone bills, gas (US only)
  • CryptoRefills - buy gift cards with BTC, phone bills, groceries
